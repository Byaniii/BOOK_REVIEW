I found subject in middle-school such as English a difficult endeavor for me not because I was unable to speak the language fluently but because the works we were given were subjective, no real metric to justify the scoring system but subjectiveness by a tutor on whether or not the criteria is met. Which is why mathematics and science have always been much more rational to me as for them there was merely one concrete answer to each problem. There was never a need for creativity, just the need to know the formula and it was simple mesmerization of those said formulas that paved high scores.
